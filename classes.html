Our Classes

At Shrimati Pushpa Devi School , we offer a wide range of academic programs designed to meet the developmental and educational needs of students from Grades 1 through 12.

Primary School (Grades 1–5)

Our primary classes focus on building strong foundations in reading, writing, mathematics, and social skills. Core subjects include:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Science
  • Arts and Crafts

Middle School(Grades 6–8)

S.P.D. PUBLIC School encourages analytical thinking and introduces students to more complex subjects. Students study:

  • English Literature & Grammar
  • Mathematics (Algebra & Geometry)
  • General Science
  • History & Civics
  • Computer Basics
  • Second Language (e.g., French or Spanish)

Girls School (Grades 6–12)

Shrimati Pushpa Devi Inter Colllege prepares students for higher education and career paths. Courses include both core subjects and electives in Science, Commerce, and Arts streams.

  • English Language & Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Arts, Humanity
  • History, Geography, Political Science
  • Information Technology

Electives & Extracurriculars

We offer a wide range of elective courses, including:

  • Music & Performing Arts
  • Debate & Public Speaking
  • Tours & Cultural Activities
  • Sports & Fitness
  • Community Service Projects
