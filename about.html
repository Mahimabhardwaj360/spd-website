Our Mission

At Shrimati Puspa Devi School, our mission is to provide a safe, inclusive, and stimulating environment where students are encouraged to reach their full potential academically, socially, and emotionally.

History

Founded in 2016, Our school has served generations of learners in our community. We are proud of our legacy of academic excellence and community involvement.

Meet Our Principal

Principal Vandana Aggrawal leads our school with over 10 years of experience in education. Her dedication to student success and school improvement is evident in every aspect of Shrimati Puspa Devi School.

Why Choose Us?

We offer a balanced curriculum, committed teachers, strong extracurricular programs, and a supportive community. Our graduates go on to succeed in top universities and careers worldwide.
